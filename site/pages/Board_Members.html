Board Members

HeatSync Labs is run by a Board of Directors, which aids in procedural and financial decisions made at HeatSync. Each officer is voted into office once a year by the membership, unless they become impeached or resign. The bylaws outline the structure of the board in more detail. All board officers must be a member in good standing, attend once-monthly board meetings, and advocate for Heatsync. If you're interested in being on the board or want to contact the board, please e-mail using board {at} heatsynclabs.org.

Board Elections

  • Elections are held annually at the April HYH meeting
  • Any member in good standing may run for any position
  • Voting is by show of hands (or electronic if virtual)
  • Terms are one year; no term limits
  • Vacancies may be filled by Board appointment until the next election
